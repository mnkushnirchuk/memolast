About Memolast

Memolast is a photo sharing application that makes it easy to collect pictures from a large group of people. You simply create an album and invite others to join. Once they’ve joined your album, they can upload their photos and videos to share with you and everyone else!

Inspiration

There were over 400 people at my wedding. Many of them I had never met before since they were friends of friends and extended family of my in-laws. I wanted to get the pictures and videos that they captured during our ceremony and reception. But how would I do that? Would I have to walk up to each person and ask them to send those photos and videos personally? I realized there was no easy way to do that, so Memolast was created to address this problem. Create a one-stop hub where everyone who attended the wedding can share their photos.

Memolast is perfect for:

  • Collecting and sharing photos from everyone at a social gathering like a wedding, birthday, baby shower, or family reunion, etc.
  • Photographers looking to share photos with their clients. Showcase your portfolio with beautiful photo galleries.
  • Keeping in touch with friends and family through shared group albums.
  • Schools looking to gather and collect photos for their yearbook and newsletters. Have your students and staff upload pictures from their field trip, sporting event, field day, staff social, prom night, and any other activities in the classroom or lab setting. This makes it easy to create community with not only your staff and students, but parents as well.
  • Creating a vibrant community within your company or organization. Keep your staff informed of events happening throughout your organization. Posts pictures and videos about the latest weekly accomplishtments, new hires, community service events, or safety incident to keep your staff up to date and engaged.
